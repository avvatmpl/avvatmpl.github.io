STAND AT


EASE

Stand at Ease is an assistive chair for the elderly who suffer from knee joint problem and
face difficulty while raising up from the sitting posture.

Why Stand At Ease?

Problem

  • Difficulty during sitting and rising from a chair.
  • Inability to bear excessive loads on the knees and hip.
  • Muscle and joint pain increases with time due to unassisted seating.

Goal

  • Gradual assistance while rising from the chair.
  • Resistive support to the occupant while sitting.

Problem Statement

  • Initial Testing with sEMG sensors on normal chair.
  • High thigh muscle activation observed while using normal chair doing stand-to-sit
    and sit-to-stand actions.

Solution / Product Innovation

  • Geriatricians interactions and user insights led us to design our chair.
  • Stand At Ease chair with patented Compliant Hinge Mechanism.

Why Stand at Ease Works?

Stand To Sit

The sharp rise at the beginning gives a great support and slows down towards the end as the user is sitting.

By this mechanism the user is not applying tension on their knees while sitting.

Sit To Stand

While standing the seat pushes the user, thus offloading the arms and reducing muscle activation.

The seat and mechanism provide resistance to sudden drop thus protecting hips and reducing thigh muscle activation.
